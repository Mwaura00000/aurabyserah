Our Brand Story

Crafted for Cozy, Soft Living

"Aura by Serah isn't just about selling candles — it's about creating experiences, moods, and memories through scent."

Aura by Serah is a luxury scented candle brand created to bring warmth, comfort, softness, and elegance into everyday spaces. Inspired by a love for calm environments, beautiful aesthetics, self-care, and the small moments that make a house feel like home, every candle is hand-poured with intention using 100% soy wax.

We carefully select our fragrances to help you relax, unwind, and elevate your space. Every single wick we light is an invitation to pause, breathe, and indulge in cozy living.
